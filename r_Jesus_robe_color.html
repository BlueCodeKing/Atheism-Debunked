Was Jesus' Robe Scarlet or Purple?

Claim

Matthew says the Romans put a scarlet robe on Jesus. While Mark and John say it was a purple robe.

Passages

Matthew 27:28

Mark 15:17

John 19:2

Short Response

The Greek word for purple used by Mark and John can mean purple, or bluish red.

Long Response

The Greek word "πορφυροῦς" (porphurous) can mean purple, or a bluish red.1 In other words, the cloak may have been more red than blue. Allowing it to be both purple and red depending on who you asked.
