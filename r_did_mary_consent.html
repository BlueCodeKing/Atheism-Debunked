Mary Didn't Consent to Being Pregnant with Jesus

Claim

God never got Mary's consent to make her pregnant.

Passage

Luke 1:31

Short Response

Actualy she did.

Long Response

Mary explicitly consents to being pregnant with Jesus in Luke 1:38.

[Luke 1:38] "Then Mary said, “Here am I, the servant of the Lord; let it be with me according to your word.” Then the angel departed from her."

Objections

Objection: "Mary only consented after the angel Gabriel told her what would happen."

Response: If God is all-knowing, He would know if Mary wanted a baby or not ahead of time. Judging from the quoted passage, it seems she did. Just like most women of her time.
